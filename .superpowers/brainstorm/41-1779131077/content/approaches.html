3 Ways to Build the Full Overhaul

Each phase ships playable value. Pick the order that feels right.

A

Content → Upgrades → Meta (Recommended)

Phase 1: New rock types, bosses, hazard events — levels feel unique immediately.

Phase 2: In-run upgrade picks between levels — replayability jumps.

Phase 3: Moon Crystals + persistent upgrade tree — long-term hook.

Pros

  • Each phase feels complete on its own
  • Content stress-tests the systems you build later

Cons

  • No long-term hook until Phase 3
B

Upgrades → Content → Meta

Phase 1: In-run upgrade picks + 5 new weapon powers.

Phase 2: Bosses, hazards, new rock types fill the richer system.

Phase 3: Meta-progression, save system, currency.

Pros

  • Huge replayability gain from day 1

Cons

  • Upgrades need varied content to shine — risk of feeling thin early
C

All Three — Minimal Viable Slices

Ship small slices of all 3 pillars at once: 2 new rock types + 3 upgrade picks + basic currency saving.

Pros

  • Full vision visible early

Cons

  • Nothing feels deep — risk of feeling half-baked across the board
