Phase 1 — New Content

Make levels feel different every play. Three content pillars.

🪨 New Rock Types

Comet

3× faster than normal rock. One hit destroys it. Leaves a glowing fire trail. High score reward.

Visual: bright white streak, blue glow

Armored Rock

Deflector bounces off harmlessly. Needs 2 blaster hits to destroy. Shows cracking on first hit.

Visual: metallic grey shell, crack on hit

Magnetic Rock

Pulls nearby rocks toward it as it travels. Creates unpredictable clusters. Destroy it to break pull.

Visual: purple glow, dotted field ring

Swarm Rock

Splits into 6 tiny fast fragments on hit instead of 2–3. Each fragment does minor damage.

Visual: orange, slightly larger than normal

Phantom Rock

Invisible until it enters moon orbit range. No trajectory preview shown. Audio cue only.

Visual: appears with flicker effect on reveal

💀 Boss Waves

Boss — appears at end of Level 3 and Level 5

Mega Rock — 5× normal size, health bar shown on HUD. Takes 5 blaster hits. Deflector slows but can't redirect. Spawns 2 regular rocks on each blaster hit. Destroying it grants +3 Starnet + score bonus.

Gravity Boss (Level 5 only) — orbits Earth, pulling all other rocks toward it. Must be destroyed from satellite blaster angle only (moon shots deflect off). 8 hits to kill.

⚡ Hazard Events

Random event triggers once per level, announced with banner. Lasts 10–20 seconds.

Solar Flare

Satellite blaster disabled for 15s. Moon deflector only. Forces defensive play.

Meteor Shower

Spawn rate doubles for 12s. All rocks are level 1–2 (manageable but overwhelming).

Gravity Surge

Earth gravity doubles for 10s. All rocks accelerate faster toward Earth. Trajectory lines shift.

Rogue Moon

Moon orbit speed triples for 15s. Hard to aim deflector shots. Extra points for deflections during event.
