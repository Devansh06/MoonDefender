Rock Types

Normal Rock Small rocks destroyed in one hit. Bigger rocks split into fragments when hit. Deflector pushes them off course. Max damage 35.
Comet 3× speed with fire trail. Any single hit destroys it. Worth +150 bonus pts. Spawns from Level 2.
Armored Rock Max 1 active. Deflector: 2 hits to redirect (uncracked). Blaster first hit cracks it — then 1 deflector hit deflects. Blaster second hit destroys.
Magnetic Rock Drags nearby rocks toward it — shown by the dotted ring. Deflect or blast it normally to stop the pull.
Healing Rock Deflect or blast = lost! Activate Starnet while it's inside the ring to capture it and heal 33% damage.
Catastrophe Rock Levels 5 & 10. Timer pauses. 3 rocks orbit it (Lv 10 adds 2 comets that fly free on death). 2 blaster or starnet hits crack the armor — one more destroys it. Each hit spawns extra rocks. Starnet on uncracked armor = boomerangs back! Hitting Earth = 50 damage.
